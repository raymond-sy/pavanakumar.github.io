Pavanakumar Mohanamuraly

Marie Curie ESR (PhD candidate)

Queen Mary, University of London

Biography

I am a PhD student at the SEMS CFD optimisation group. My research focuses on Adjoint based robust aerodynamic shape design under geometry induced uncertanities. I develope and carry out my research using our groups in-house CFD code STAMPS.

Interests

  • Uncertanity quantification
  • Adjoint methods
  • Aero-acoustic design

Education

  • MS in Aerospace Engineering, 2010

    Pennsylvania State University

  • B.Tech in Aerospace Engineering, 2005

    Madras Institute of Technology
